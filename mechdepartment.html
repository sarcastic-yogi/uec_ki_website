Department of Mechanical Engineering

Ujjain Engineering College, Indore road, Ujjain-456010



Mechanical Engineering Department is one of the oldest departments of the institute.Initially there were only three departments in the college and Mechanical departmnet was one of them. It is also one of the biggest departments of the institute with student strength of about 280 which will touch approximately 540 in next few years.

While managing this increased strength which is an urgent need of the nation, department is committed to well being and all round development of its students. The department currently runs one undergraduate programme and one post graduate programme: B. Tech. (Mechanical Engineering) M. Tech. (Industrial Engineering and Management)
